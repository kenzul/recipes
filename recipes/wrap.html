Kebab Wrap

Description

This delicious wrap kebab features juicy marinated meat, fresh vegetables, and a flavorful sauce all rolled into a soft flatbread. It's a satisfying and portable meal, perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flatbreads or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup garlic or yogurt sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the sliced meat to the marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best results).
  3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and cook the meat until browned and cooked through.
  4. Warm the flatbreads in a pan or oven.
  5. Lay out each flatbread and add cooked meat, lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and red onion.
  6. Drizzle with garlic or yogurt sauce.
  7. Roll the wrap tightly and serve warm.
